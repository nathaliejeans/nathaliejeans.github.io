Hi, I'm Nathalie!


I want to build ethical, fair, and people-first technology by transforming something traditionally robotic into something emotionally meaningful. When creating, I value whether a product fulfills its purpose in the larger system, and believe that user experience is key to forming those human-based connections.

I'm currently a rising studying Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm also a UX Designer for CMU CS Academy, a free world-class high school computer science curriculum with 61,000+ students from 1,000+ schools. Previously, I've interned as a Machine Learning Scientist at Integrate.ai, where I worked on creating a metric to quantify the customer centricity of clients' business decisions. I also co-led the first cohort of high school girls participating in SheEO's funding cycle, voting on women-led startups to receive grants.

Various articles of mine have been in Medium's Towards Data Science and Data Driven Investor publications. The Backpropagation Algorithm Demystified was featured on the Practical AI Podcast and in KDnuggets. I spoke about generating music with AI at the Venturing into Tech conference at the University of Toronto, St. George.

You can find me upcycling clothes and going on outdoor adventures!
